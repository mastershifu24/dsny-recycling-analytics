Rank & optimize garbage truck routes

Goal: ordered stops using road driving times (OSRM) when possible, plus datasets for constraints. Borough tonnage in the main app is separate.

Basic stop optimizer (built-in)

Paste JSON: lat, lng, optional label, and optional borough + community_district (NYC community board number, 1–18) to bias order toward higher garbage-tonnage districts using latest published DSNY monthly data (Open Data). Nearest-neighbor order. Optional: Google live traffic (needs GOOGLE_MAPS_API_KEY on the server). NYC DOT truck rules are not baked into Google—use Truck-aware for links + disclaimer.
