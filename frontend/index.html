What this is

Quick borough-level garbage & recycling trends from published city numbers—no long forms. For pickup day, we point you to the official address lookup (you need a real street address).

Routes: borough tonnage data does not include truck paths. A separate stop-order helper uses road-network driving times (OSRM)—not live traffic or DSNY dispatch. DOT maps & links
