DSNY Recycling Analytics

For sanitation crews & truck drivers · voice or text · NYC open data

Problem: Wrong trash in recycling ruins loads—and stopping to write it all up is brutal on a route. This app: use voice or text to pull city data (borough refuse trends, context) without forms. It does not replace paperwork for enforcement; it gives fast facts from open data and links for pickup-day questions (address-based).

Try asking:

  • “Bronx refuse trend month over month”
  • “When is garbage day in Queens” (we link the official schedule—you need an address for your stop)

Data: NYC DSNY Monthly Tonnage (open data)

Photo check (demo): point camera at a pile—needs GEMINI_API_KEY on the server. Citation output is a stub, not a real ticket.
